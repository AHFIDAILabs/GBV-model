Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How does this tool work?

The tool applies machine learning to analyze demographic, social, and contextual data. It predicts vulnerability to GBV, provides a confidence score, highlights contributing factors, and generates tailored recommendations.

Q2: What makes this model different from traditional approaches?

Unlike general assessments, the model offers a data-driven, individualized prediction with a confidence score. It also explains the top factors influencing vulnerability, giving users clear and actionable insights.

Q3: How does the model explain its predictions?

The model uses SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) to show which features most influenced the prediction. This makes the results transparent and helps users understand the key drivers of vulnerability.

Q4: Does the model replace human decision-making?

No. The model is a supportive tool that provides evidence-based insights. It complements professional judgment and helps policymakers, NGOs, and service providers make informed decisions.

Q5: Is my data saved or shared?

No. The tool does not store or share personal data. Inputs are used only for generating predictions and are not retained after use.
